About Us

Grant Audit is an international network of firms providing audit, tax and consulting services. Grant Audit offices in 154 countries employ 200,000 employees. Each company is an independent legal entity.

The total revenue of Grant Audit member firms for the year ended September 30, 2017 amounted to $ 26.4 billion. USA.

The three fundamental principles of Grant Audit are the high quality of the services provided, a deep understanding of the problems of our customers and a holistic and consistent approach to doing business. It is these principles, which reflect the unified laws of the company's activities around the world, that provide guidance to our specialists in the provision of audit services and advice on financial and tax issues. Our goal is to help everyone we work with improve, be it our employees, customers or society as a whole. This is how we intend to strengthen our reputation as the best company in the market.
